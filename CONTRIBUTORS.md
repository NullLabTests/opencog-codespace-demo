# Contributors

This repository documents a running deployment of the **OpenCog AtomSpace**
and its surrounding ecosystem. The software, vision, and community it
showcases are the work of hundreds of contributors over two decades.

## Getting Involved

The OpenCog community is active and welcoming to new contributors.

| Channel | Link | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Discord** | https://discord.gg/vxPc6sz | Daily chat — most active community hub |
| **Mailing List** | opencog@googlegroups.com | Long-form discussion, announcements |
| **GitHub Issues** | https://github.com/opencog/atomspace/issues | Bug reports, feature requests |
| **Wiki** | https://wiki.opencog.org | Documentation, tutorials, concepts |
| **Blog** | https://blog.opencog.org | Project updates, research posts |
| **Reddit** | https://reddit.com/r/opencog | Community discussion |
| **MeTTa Tutorials** | https://metta-lang.dev/docs/learn/learn.html | Learn the successor language |

**Quick start for contributors:**
1. Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/vxPc6sz)
2. Browse open issues on [AtomSpace](https://github.com/opencog/atomspace/issues) or [Hyperon](https://github.com/trueagi-io/hyperon-wasm/issues)
3. Submit PRs — all repositories accept contributions
4. Contact **Linas Vepstas** (linasvepstas@gmail.com) for developer onboarding to OpenCog Classic
5. Explore [SingularityNET Deep Funding](https://deepfunding.ai) for grant opportunities
